# Cooking-Game
Repository for the GDD109 final project.

Art Assets: Created by me!!
Sound effects: Made using Chiptone. https://sfbgames.itch.io/chiptone
Music from Pixabay (https://pixabay.com/music/search/game%20background/), by Tatamusic https://pixabay.com/music/video-games-game-gaming-minecraft-background-music-377647/ 

 Moo-Moo's Pancakeria is very different from what I first intended. It was meant to be a stereotypical resturant game where you cook the food and give specfic orders to customers. However, due to time constraints and lack of available guides, I turned my game into an endless feeder, similar to something like Bunny Pancake Kitty Milkshake. In this new version, you have to try and feed the customers as quickly as possible within 3 seconds, or else they lose. When a customer is fed, the timer is reset. So overall it's pretty fast-paced!

 I can't say this is the vision I firstly had, but I'm proud of the fact I even finished this, as I was really starting to give up.
